## 1. 🌍 Introduction

At Relax.ng, our mission is to take the stress out of daily living by delivering fast, reliable, and seamless lifestyle services. From home care and maintenance to wellness and personal needs, we help Nigerians save time, enjoy convenience, and live with peace of mind.

**Vision**

To become Africa’s leading lifestyle companion, setting the standard for modern convenience and trusted service delivery. We aim to create a future where households and individuals live with more ease, freedom, and joy.

---

## 2. 🔖 Logo

**Primary Logo**

- Wordmark in **Cirqua** (default logo)
- Use full logo whenever possible

**Alternate Versions**

- Icon/mark-only version (for limited spaces)
- Single-color version (for minimal layouts)

**Usage Rules**

✅ Maintain clear space equal to the height of “r”

✅ Minimum size: 100px (digital), 25mm (print)

❌ Do not distort, recolor, or add effects

❌ Avoid clashing backgrounds

---

## 3. 🎨 Color Palette

**Primary Colors**

- Deep Green – `#13532E`
- Warm Beige – `#EEE6D6`

**Secondary Colors**

- Vibrant Orange – `#FC7301`
- Neutral White – `#FFFFFF`
- Neutral Black – `#000000`

**Usage Guidelines**

- Green + Beige dominate layouts
- Black, White, and Orange are accents

---

## 4. ✍️ Typography

**Primary Typeface (Logo & Headlines)**

- Cirqua – bold, artistic, playful
    - Headlines, logo, and major titles only

**Secondary Typeface (Body & Supporting Text)**

- Lato – modern, friendly, highly legible
    - Subheadings, body text, captions

**Hierarchy**

- Logo: Cirqua
- Headline: Cirqua Bold
- Subheading: Lato Medium
- Body: Lato Regular
- Emphasis: Lato Italic

---

## 5. 📸 Imagery & Icons

**Photography Style**

- Natural light, calm tones, minimal clutter
- Real people, authentic moments of calm
    
    ❌ Avoid harsh contrasts, dark tones, or staged stock photos
    

**Iconography**

- Simple line icons with rounded corners
- Use brand colors for emphasis

---

## 6. 🗣 Brand Voice & Tone

**Style**

- Calm, friendly, supportive
- Conversational yet professional

**Do’s**

✅ Use positive, uplifting language

✅ Keep sentences short and clear

**Don’ts**

❌ Use jargon or negativity

❌ Over-complicate messaging

---

## 7. 📲 Applications

**Website & Digital**

- White space is essential
- Logo top-left preferred
- Text must always be high-contrast

**Social Media**

- Profile: Logo as display picture
- Posts: Cirqua for headlines, Lato for body text
- Style: Clean, uncluttered, calm

**Print & Merchandise**

- Use only official brand colors
- Maintain logo proportions, no stretching
